ABOUT US

SINGLE MANUFACTURERS OF APP IN RUSSIA
Atactic polypropylene is a derivative of polypropylene and belongs to thermoplastics by its characteristics. It has a number of useful properties and can be used in various industrial fields.
OUR APP IS USED BY THE COMPANIES "TECHNONIKOL", "TEGOLA", "ORGKROVLYA", "VZSM" AND OTHERS.
The abbreviation APP in the Russian market is not familiar to everyone, under the APP is meant: polymer based on polypropylene, bitumen modifier based on APP, pure APP, TPM (thermoplastic modifier). The analogue of our APP on the market is Westoplast.
A WIDE RANGE OF MASTICS, PBB, ROLL ROOFING WATERPROOFING MATERIALS ARE PRODUCED ON THE BASIS OF APP
Modification of bitumen is one of the main directions of the rational use of oxidized APP. For bitumen modification, APP- and SBS-modifiers are used (SBS, styrene-butadiene-styrene, is the closest substitute for APP, but its price is much higher).
